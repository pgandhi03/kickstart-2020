t=int(input())          
for pp in range(1,t+1):
    n,b=map(int,input().split())
    a=list(map(int,input().split()))
    a.sort()
    tot=b
    count=0
    for i in a:
        if(tot<i):
            break
        else:
            tot-=i
            count+=1
    l=str(pp)
    print("Case #"+l+":",count)

There are N houses for sale. The i-th house costs Ai dollars to buy. You have a budget of B dollars to spend.
